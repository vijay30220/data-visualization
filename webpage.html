Pandemic Patterns: Visualizing COVID-19's Impact on Healthcare Worldwide

Introduction Objectives Features Visualizations References

Introduction

This project examines the global impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on healthcare systems, using data visualization to highlight variations in healthcare responses and provide insight into the efficacy of different pandemic-fighting strategies.

Objectives

  • To develop a dynamic web-based dashboard that shows global healthcare capacity, vaccination rates, and COVID-19 case trends.
  • To identify nations and areas in need of urgent medical attention to enable focused initiatives.
  • To evaluate the effectiveness of various immunization campaigns and public health initiatives.
  • To forecast future patterns using available data, helping to prepare for possible strain on the healthcare system.

Features

  • Healthcare System Stress Indicators
  • Vaccine Distribution Logistics
  • Impact of Economic Analysis
  • Timeline of Public Health Policy
  • Analysis of Social Determinants
  • COVID-19's Effect on Mental Health
  • Interactive Case Studies
  • Initiatives for Mobile Health (mHealth)
